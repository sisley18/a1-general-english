📝 Grammar

Verb "To Be" — Present Simple

I am → I'm a teacher.
You are → You're my friend.
He is / She is / It is → She's from Brazil.
We are → We're students.
They are → They're happy.
Questions: Are you a student? Is she from Mexico?
Negatives: I am not (I'm not) tired. He is not (isn't) a doctor.

Greetings from Rome! We are having a wonderful time. We arrived two days ago. Yesterday, we went to the Colosseum. We took many photos. For dinner, we ate real Italian pizza and drank wine. The weather is perfect. Today, we are going to visit the Vatican.

See you soon!

Love, Emma
