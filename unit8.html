Free Time and Hobbies

Learn to talk about hobbies, likes and dislikes, adverbs of frequency, and leisure activities. The final unit!

📚 Vocabulary

Hobbies and Activities

Play soccer / football
A popular team sport
"I play soccer on weekends."
Watch TV / movies
To look at a screen for entertainment
"I watch movies on Friday night."
Listen to music
To enjoy songs and music
"I listen to music every day."
Read books
To look at written words and understand them
"She reads books before bed."
Cook
To prepare food
"I like to cook Italian food."
Go swimming
To swim in a pool or the sea
"We go swimming in the summer."
Play video games
Electronic games on a computer or console
"He plays video games after school."
Take photos
To use a camera to capture images
"She loves to take photos of nature."
Go for a walk
To walk outside for pleasure
"I go for a walk in the park."
Draw / Paint
To create pictures with pencils or colors
"My sister likes to draw animals."
📝 Grammar

Like / Don't Like + Adverbs of Frequency

Like + verb-ing: I like swimming. She likes cooking. They like reading.
Don't like: I don't like running. He doesn't like dancing.
Love / Hate: I love playing soccer. She hates getting up early.
Questions: Do you like swimming? Does he like cooking?
Adverbs of Frequency:
always (100%) → usually (80%) → often (60%) → sometimes (40%) → rarely (20%) → never (0%)

I always listen to music. She sometimes plays tennis. He never watches horror movies.
💫 Key Collocations

Free Time Expressions

In my free time
"In my free time, I like reading."
On weekends
"On weekends, I play soccer with my friends."
After school / work
"After work, I go to the gym."
I'm interested in...
"I'm interested in photography."

Listening Comprehension

🔊 Audio 1: What Do You Like Doing?

📝 Transcript:

Hi, my name is Laura. In my free time, I like listening to music and reading books. I usually read before bed. On weekends, I go swimming with my friends. I love swimming! I also like cooking. I sometimes cook dinner for my family on Sundays. I don't like playing video games. I think they are boring. I never play video games.

Speaking Practice

Interview: Free Time Survey

Partner:Yes, I do. / No, I don't.
You:How often do you ______?

Partner:I hate ______!

Reading Comprehension

Weekend Activities Survey

A school in London asks one hundred students: "What do you do on weekends?" Here are the results:

Sixty students like watching TV or movies. Fifty-five students like listening to music. Forty-eight students like meeting friends. Thirty-five students like playing sports. Twenty-five students like reading. Twenty students like playing video games. Fifteen students like cooking.

The most popular weekend activity is watching TV. The least popular activity is cooking. Many students say they always use their phone on weekends too!
