Food and Drink

Learn food vocabulary, countable and uncountable nouns, "some" and "any," and how to order at a restaurant.

📚 Vocabulary

Food and Drink

Food

Bread
A basic baked food made from flour
"I eat bread for breakfast."
Rice
Small white or brown grains, very common food
"We eat rice with chicken."
Chicken
Meat from a bird
"I like chicken with salad."
Fish
An animal that lives in water, also a food
"Fish is very healthy."
Egg
Comes from a chicken, very common for breakfast
"I have two eggs for breakfast."
Fruit
Apples, oranges, bananas, etc.
"I like fruit. My favorite is banana."
Vegetables
Tomatoes, carrots, onions, etc.
"Vegetables are good for you."
Cheese
A food made from milk
"I want a cheese sandwich, please."

Drinks

Water
"Can I have a glass of water, please?"
Coffee / Tea
"I drink coffee in the morning."
Juice
"I like orange juice."
Milk
"Children drink milk every day."
📝 Grammar

Countable & Uncountable Nouns — Some / Any

Countable: an apple, two eggs, three tomatoes
Uncountable: water, rice, bread, milk, cheese
Some (positive): There is some milk. I have some apples.
Any (negative & questions): Is there any bread? There isn't any juice.
A / An: I want an apple. She eats a sandwich.
How much / How many: How much water? How many eggs?

Listening Comprehension

🔊 Audio 1: Ordering at a Restaurant

📝 Transcript:

Waiter: Good afternoon. Welcome to the Blue Café. Are you ready to order?

Woman: Yes, please. I would like a chicken sandwich and a salad.

Waiter: Of course. And to drink?

Woman: I would like an orange juice, please.

Waiter: And for you, sir?

Man: I would like the fish with rice, please. And a glass of water.

Waiter: Very good. Anything else?

Man: Yes, can we have some bread, please?

Waiter: Of course. Your food will be ready in ten minutes.

Man: Thank you very much.

Eating Around the World

In Japan, people eat a lot of rice and fish. A typical breakfast is rice, fish, and soup. In Italy, people love pasta and pizza. They also eat a lot of bread and cheese. Italian coffee is very famous.

In Mexico, people eat tacos, beans, and rice. They drink a lot of fruit juice. Mexican food is very spicy. In England, a typical breakfast is eggs, toast, sausages, and tea. English people drink a lot of tea.

All around the world, people eat different food, but everyone likes good food!
