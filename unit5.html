My Home

Learn rooms of a house, furniture vocabulary, prepositions of place, and "there is / there are."

📚 Vocabulary

Rooms and Furniture

Rooms of the House

Living room
The room where you relax and watch TV
"The sofa is in the living room."
Kitchen
The room where you cook food
"My mother cooks in the kitchen."
Bedroom
The room where you sleep
"I have a big bedroom."
Bathroom
The room with a shower and toilet
"The bathroom is upstairs."
Dining room
The room where you eat meals
"We eat dinner in the dining room."
Garden / Yard
The outdoor area around a house
"The children play in the garden."

Furniture

Table / Chair
"There is a table and four chairs."
Sofa / Couch
"The sofa is very comfortable."
Bed
"My bed is next to the window."
Wardrobe / Closet
"My clothes are in the wardrobe."
Fridge
"The milk is in the fridge."
Lamp
"There is a lamp on the desk."
📝 Grammar

There is / There are — Prepositions of Place

There is (singular): There is a lamp on the table.
There are (plural): There are two bedrooms in my house.
Negative: There isn't a garage. There aren't any pictures.
Questions: Is there a garden? Are there any chairs?
Prepositions:
on (on the table), in (in the box), under (under the bed),
next to (next to the window), between (between the chairs), in front of (in front of the sofa)
🎧 Listening

Listening Comprehension

🔊 Audio 1: Describing an Apartment

📝 Transcript:

Welcome to my apartment! It is not very big, but I like it. There are three rooms. First, there is the living room. There is a sofa, a small table, and a television. The sofa is in front of the television. Next to the living room, there is the kitchen. It is small. There is a fridge, a stove, and a table with two chairs. My bedroom is next to the kitchen. There is a bed, a wardrobe, and a desk. The desk is next to the window. There is a lamp on the desk. The bathroom is between the bedroom and the kitchen. There is not a garden, but there is a small balcony.

True or False?

  • 1. The apartment is very big. _____
  • 2. The sofa is in front of the television. _____
  • 3. There are three chairs in the kitchen. _____
  • 4. There is a garden. _____
  • 5. The desk is next to the window. _____

Partner:Is there a garden?

📖 Reading

Reading Comprehension

Two Different Homes

Lisa's house: Lisa lives in a big house in the country. There are four bedrooms, a large kitchen, and two bathrooms. There is a beautiful garden with flowers and trees. The living room is very big. There is a fireplace and a comfortable sofa. Lisa loves her house.

Marco's apartment: Marco lives in a small apartment in the city. There is one bedroom, a kitchen, a bathroom, and a living room. There is not a garden, but there is a small balcony. The apartment is on the fifth floor. From the balcony, Marco can see the park. He likes his apartment because it is close to his school.

1. Where does Lisa live — city or country? _____
2. How many bedrooms does Lisa have? _____
3. Does Marco have a garden? _____
4. What can Marco see from his balcony? _____
5. Why does Marco like his apartment? _____
