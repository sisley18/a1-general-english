📘 Unit 6 of 8

Around Town

Learn places in a town, ask for and give directions, use prepositions of movement, and practice with "can / can't."

📚 Vocabulary

Places in Town

Supermarket
A large store that sells food and other products
"I buy food at the supermarket."
Hospital
A place where sick people go
"The hospital is on Main Street."
School
A place where children study
"The school is next to the park."
Bank
A place where you keep your money
"The bank opens at nine o'clock."
Post office
A place where you send letters
"I go to the post office to send a letter."
Pharmacy / Drugstore
A place where you buy medicine
"The pharmacy is across from the bank."
Restaurant
A place where you eat food
"There is a good restaurant on Park Avenue."
Park
An area with grass, trees, and benches
"I walk in the park every morning."

Giving Directions

Go straight
"Go straight for two blocks."
Turn left / Turn right
"Turn left at the traffic light."
It's on the corner
"The bank is on the corner."
Across from / Opposite
"The school is across from the park."
📝 Grammar

Can / Can't — Asking for Help

Can (ability / permission): I can swim. Can you help me?
Can't (cannot): I can't speak French. She can't drive.
Asking for directions: Excuse me, can you tell me where the bank is?
Polite requests: Can I have a coffee, please? Can you repeat that, please?
🎧 Listening

Listening Comprehension

🔊 Audio 1: Asking for Directions

📝 Transcript:

Tourist: Excuse me. Can you tell me where the post office is?

Person: Yes, of course. Go straight on this street for two blocks. Then turn right at the traffic light. The post office is on the left, next to the pharmacy.

Tourist: Is it far from here?

Person: No, it is about five minutes on foot.

Tourist: Thank you very much!

Person: You are welcome.

Answer:

  • 1. How many blocks straight? _____
  • 2. Turn left or right? _____
  • 3. The post office is next to the _____.
  • 4. How many minutes on foot? _____

📖 Reading

Reading Comprehension

A Day in the City

Today is Saturday. Pablo and his sister Ana go to the city center. First, they go to the bank. The bank is on Main Street. Then they walk to the supermarket. The supermarket is across from the park. They buy some fruit and vegetables.

After the supermarket, they go to a restaurant for lunch. The restaurant is between the bookstore and the pharmacy. They eat pizza and drink orange juice. After lunch, they walk to the park. There are many people in the park. Children play, and dogs run on the grass.

Pablo and Ana come home at five o'clock. They have a very good day.

1. What day is it? _____
2. Where is the bank? _____
3. What do they buy at the supermarket? _____
4. Where is the restaurant? _____
5. What time do they come home? _____
