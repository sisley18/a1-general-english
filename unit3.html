📘 Unit 3 of 8

My Daily Routine

Learn to describe your daily activities, tell the time, and use the Present Simple tense with everyday verbs.

📚 Vocabulary

Daily Activities

Wake up
To stop sleeping
"I wake up at seven o'clock."
Have breakfast
To eat the first meal of the day
"I have breakfast at half past seven."
Go to work / school
To travel to your job or school
"I go to work by bus."
Have lunch
To eat the midday meal
"I have lunch at twelve thirty."
Come home
To return to your house
"I come home at five o'clock."
Have dinner
To eat the evening meal
"We have dinner at eight o'clock."
Take a shower
To wash your body with water
"I take a shower every morning."
Go to bed
To go to sleep
"I go to bed at ten thirty."

Telling the Time

O'clock
"It is three o'clock." (3:00)
Half past
"It is half past six." (6:30)
Quarter past
"It is quarter past nine." (9:15)
Quarter to
"It is quarter to eight." (7:45)
📝 Grammar

Present Simple — Daily Routines

I / You / We / They → I get up at seven. They eat lunch at noon.
He / She / It → She gets up at six. He eats lunch at one.
Negatives: I don't work on Sundays. She doesn't drive to work.
Questions: Do you work on Saturdays? Does he take the bus?
🎧 Listening

Listening Comprehension

🔊 Audio 1: A Typical Day

📝 Transcript:

My name is David. Let me tell you about my typical day. I wake up at six thirty every morning. I take a shower and get dressed. Then I have breakfast. I usually eat toast and drink coffee. I leave the house at seven thirty. I go to work by train. I start work at eight thirty. I have lunch at twelve thirty. I usually eat a sandwich. I finish work at five o'clock. I come home at six o'clock. I have dinner with my family at seven. After dinner, I watch television or read a book. I go to bed at ten thirty.

Complete David's schedule:

  • 7:30 — _____
  • 8:30 — _____

  • 5:00 — _____
  • 7:00 — _____
  • 10:30 — _____

📖 Reading

Reading Comprehension

Maria's Busy Day

Maria is a student. She lives in Cordoba, Argentina. She wakes up at six o'clock every day. She takes a shower and gets dressed. She has breakfast at six thirty. She usually eats cereal and drinks milk.

Maria goes to university by bus. Her classes start at eight o'clock. She has lunch at the university cafeteria at twelve. In the afternoon, she studies at the library.

Maria comes home at five o'clock. She helps her mother with dinner. They eat together at eight o'clock. After dinner, Maria does her homework. She goes to bed at eleven o'clock. She is always very tired!

1. What time does Maria wake up? _____
2. How does she go to university? _____
3. Where does she have lunch? _____
4. What does she do after dinner? _____
5. What time does she go to bed? _____
