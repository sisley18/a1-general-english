📘 Unit 7 of 8

Clothes and Shopping

Learn clothes vocabulary, colors, sizes, how to shop, and the Present Continuous tense (what are you wearing now?).

📚 Vocabulary

Clothes

T-shirt
A casual top with short sleeves
"I am wearing a blue T-shirt."
Jeans
Casual pants made of denim
"She is wearing jeans and sneakers."
Dress
A piece of clothing for women/girls
"The red dress is very beautiful."
Jacket / Coat
You wear it when it is cold
"Put on your jacket. It is cold outside."
Shoes / Sneakers
You wear them on your feet
"I need new shoes for school."
Skirt
A piece of clothing that hangs from the waist
"She has a black skirt."
Shirt
A more formal top with buttons
"He is wearing a white shirt."
Hat / Cap
You wear it on your head
"He always wears a baseball cap."

Colors

Red, Blue, Green, Yellow
"I like the blue shirt."
Black, White, Brown, Pink
"She has a pink dress."
Orange, Purple, Gray
"The orange jacket is nice."

Shopping Phrases

How much is it?
"How much is this T-shirt?"
Can I try it on?
"Can I try on the blue one?"
It's too big / too small
"This is too small. Do you have a large?"
I'll take it
"I like it. I'll take it!"
📝 Grammar

Present Continuous — What Are You Wearing?

I am wearing (I'm wearing) a blue T-shirt.
She is wearing (She's wearing) a red dress.
They are wearing (They're wearing) jeans.
Questions: What are you wearing? Is she wearing a hat?
Negatives: I'm not wearing shoes. He isn't wearing a jacket.
Present Continuous = NOW: I am sitting. She is reading. They are talking.
🎧 Listening

Listening Comprehension

🔊 Audio 1: Shopping for Clothes

📝 Transcript:

Shop assistant: Good morning. Can I help you?

Customer: Yes, I am looking for a T-shirt.

Shop assistant: What color would you like?

Customer: Blue, please.

Shop assistant: What size?

Customer: Medium, please.

Shop assistant: Here you are.

Customer: How much is it?

Shop assistant: It is fifteen dollars.

Customer: Can I try it on?

Shop assistant: Of course. The fitting room is over there.

Customer: It fits perfectly. I'll take it.

Answer:

  • 1. What is the customer looking for? _____
  • 2. What color? _____
  • 3. What size? _____
  • 4. How much is it? _____
  • 5. Does the customer buy it? _____

📖 Reading

Reading Comprehension

Fashion Around the World

People wear different clothes in different countries. In Japan, people sometimes wear a kimono for special events. A kimono is a long, beautiful dress. In Scotland, men sometimes wear a kilt. A kilt looks like a skirt.

In cold countries like Russia, people wear warm coats, hats, and boots in winter. In hot countries like Brazil, people wear T-shirts, shorts, and sandals.

Today, many people around the world wear jeans and T-shirts. Jeans are the most popular clothes in the world! Young people like sneakers and caps. Everyone has their own style.

1. What is a kimono? _____
2. What do people wear in cold countries? _____
3. What are the most popular clothes in the world? _____
4. What do people wear in hot countries? _____
