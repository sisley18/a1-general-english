📘 Unit 2 of 8

My Family

Learn family vocabulary, possessive adjectives, and how to talk about your family members, their ages, and occupations.

📚 Vocabulary

Family Members

Mother / Mom
Your female parent
"My mother is a teacher."
Father / Dad
Your male parent
"My father works in an office."
Sister
A female sibling
"I have one sister. Her name is Laura."
Brother
A male sibling
"My brother is twelve years old."
Grandmother / Grandma
Your parent's mother
"My grandmother lives in the country."
Grandfather / Grandpa
Your parent's father
"My grandfather is seventy years old."
Husband / Wife
A married partner
"Her husband is a doctor."
Son / Daughter
A male or female child
"They have two sons and one daughter."
📝 Grammar

Possessive Adjectives

My → My name is Ana.
Your → What is your name?
His → His father is tall.
Her → Her mother is a nurse.
Our → Our family is big.
Their → Their children are young.
Its → The dog loves its ball.
💫 Key Collocations

Talking About Family

I have... / I don't have...
"I have two brothers. I don't have a sister."
How many... do you have?
"How many brothers do you have?"
He/She looks like...
"She looks like her mother."
My family is big/small
"My family is big. I have four brothers!"
🎧 Listening

Listening Comprehension

🔊 Audio 1: Describing a Family

📝 Transcript:

Hello, my name is Emma. I want to tell you about my family. My family is not very big. I have a mother, a father, and one brother. My mother's name is Helen. She is forty-five years old. She is a nurse. My father's name is Robert. He is forty-eight years old. He is a bus driver. My brother's name is James. He is sixteen years old. He is a student. We have a dog. Its name is Max. I love my family very much.

Answer the questions:

  • 1. How many brothers does Emma have? _____
  • 2. What is her mother's job? _____
  • 3. How old is her father? _____
  • 4. What is the dog's name? _____

📖 Reading

Reading Comprehension

The Garcia Family

The Garcia family lives in Buenos Aires, Argentina. There are five people in the family. The father's name is Marcos. He is forty-two years old. He is a mechanic. The mother's name is Silvia. She is thirty-nine years old. She is a secretary.

They have three children. The oldest is Diego. He is eighteen years old. He is a university student. The second child is Camila. She is fifteen. She goes to high school. The youngest is little Mateo. He is only five years old. He goes to kindergarten.

The family also has two pets: a cat named Luna and a fish named Nemo. They are a happy family.

Questions:

1. Where does the Garcia family live? _____
2. How many children do Marcos and Silvia have? _____
3. How old is Camila? _____
4. What is the cat's name? _____
5. What does Marcos do? _____
